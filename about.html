My Story

Hello! I'm Gideon Nzaka, a passionate Microbiologist student at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology. My journey in the fascinating world of microbiology has been driven by curiosity and a desire to understand the invisible forces that shape our health and environment.

Growing up with a deep appreciation for both science and culinary arts, I've found a unique intersection where my academic pursuits meet my passion for food. This portfolio showcases not only my academic journey but also my culinary creations that bring joy and nourishment.

When I'm not in the laboratory studying microorganisms, you'll find me experimenting with recipes, particularly my specialties: roasted beef and pilau. I believe that understanding the science behind food preparation enhances the culinary experience, creating a beautiful blend of art and science.
